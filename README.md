## Habits To Go -- Smart Habit Tracker
- intelligent habit tracker designed to help you build better habits without wasting time in manual logging
- Combine manual tracking with automatic habit tracking from your favorite apps

## Features
- **Manual Tracking**: Quickly mark habits as done with a single tap
- **Automatic Tracking**: Seamlessly track habits by integrating different systems
  - **Coding habits**: Track GitHub commits
  - **Calendar habits**: Detect hobby or productivity sessions from your calendar
  - **Health & Fitness**: Track sleep, steps, workouts via Apple Health
- **Streaks & Progess**: Visualize your habit streaks and weekly / montly progress
- **Share habits with friends**: Users can share specific habits with friends who also use the app

## How it works
1. Add your habbit: Choose from pre-defined habits or create your own
2. Connect Integrations: Grant access to supported integrations
3. Track automatically or manually: your process will be udpated automatically or you can log it manually
4. Visualize your growth: streaks & charts help you stay motivated
